--- title: Verbs ending in "are" plural - A1 - Italian layout: page child: Languages fold: Italian --- To build the forms of regular verbs such as "lavorare" we first need, as usual, to drop the last 3 letters "-are". Then, we add to the stem ("lavor-") the following endings. | Personal pronoun | Verb "lavorare" | | ---------------- | --------------- | | Noi | lavor - **iamo** | | Voi | lavor - **ate** | | Loro | lavor - **ano** | When a verb stem ends with the letter "-i", such as studiare (studi) and mangiare (mangi), we don't need to add another "-i" for the "Noi" form.
