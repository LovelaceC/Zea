--- title: Introduction to nouns - A1 - Italian layout: page child: Languages fold: Italian --- As in many other languages, in italian all nouns (words indicating people, animals, and objects) have a gender, meaning that they are either masculine or feminine. - Masculine: uomo (man), gatto (cat) - Feminine: donna (woman), birra (beer) Most masculine nouns end with the letter "**o**" whereas most feminine nouns end with the letter "**a**".
