--- title: The verb volere - A1 - Italian layout: page child: Languages fold: Italian --- In this unit, you will learn how to say what you want by using the verb "**volere**" (to want). | Italian | Meaning | | ------- | ------- | | Volere | To want | | Io voglio | I want | | Tu vuoi | You want | | lui, lei, Lei vuole | He, She wants | | Noi vogliamo | We want | | Voi volete | You (plural) want | | Loro vogliono | They want | Notice how the verb after "**volere**" is always in the **base form**, namely the form ending in "are", "ere" or "ire". - **Vogliamo prenotare** un taxi.
